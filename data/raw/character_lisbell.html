BUILD

WEAPON

Wanewraith

Consonance Weapon does not inherit stats from melee/ranged weapon, and instead have their own stats. Only certain Weapon Skills like Character ATK, Skill Damage, Skill Effeciency, Skill Range, Skill Duration, can be applied to the character. Therefore, Wanewraith and Blade Amberglow is Best because it boost Skill DMG and Character ATK respectively. Wanewraith is chosen here because Skill DMG is less saturated compared to ATK which have multipler sources. Player can leave these weapons at Level 1, and simply Smelt it to the max.

(Optional) Fenrir's Continuity - Trammei can boost Combo Duration when slotted into Melee Weapon.

Embla Inflorescence

Skill Range bonus, and Skill DMG buff up to 70%, to boost Lisbell's ULT damage.
