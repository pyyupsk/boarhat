Ultimate - DMG Heaven-Earth Divination

When Zhiliu has fewer than 2 [Talismans], deals Electro Damage to enemies within range. When Zhiliu has 2 [Talismans], this skill is replaced with another skill based on the type of [Talisman].

[Thunder's Oracle Peal]: When Zhiliu have 2 [Talismans: Zhen], [Heaven-Earth Divination] is replaced with [Thunder's Oracle Peal].
[Thunder's Oracle Peal]: Consumes all [Talismans], dealing Electro Damage to enemies in a straight line ahead.

[Myriad Things Thrive]: When Zhiliu have 1 [Talismans: Zhen] and 1 [Talismans: Xun], [Heaven-Earth Divination] is replaced with [Myriad Things Thrive].
[Myriad Things Thrive]: Consumes all [Talismans], dealing periodic damage to enemies within range in front, then creates a [Four Realms Align] field at the location.
[Four Realms Align]: Selects several enemies within range every 3s and inflict 1 strike of [Zhen-Xun Convergence] on them.
[Zhen-Xun Convergence]: Deals AOE Electro Damage

[Fortune's Clarity]: When Zhiliu have 2 [Talismans: Xun], [Heaven-Earth Divination] is replaced with [Fortune's Clarity].
[Fortune's Clarity]: Consumes all [Talismans] to deal Electro Damage to enemies around and increase Zhiliu's Skill Damage.
